# Big-ass notation

- sum         : `\sum`
- product     : `\prod`
- coproduct   : `\coprod`
- union       : `\bigcup`
- intersection: `\bigcap`


#### inline 
$$\prod _{i=1} ^{n} {x_i}$$

$$\sum _{i=1} ^{n} {x_i}$$

#### block 
$$\displaystyle\prod _{i=1} ^{n} {x_i}$$

$$\displaystyle\sum _{i=1} ^{n} {x_i}$$


### geometric mean
$$
\displaystyle{
  \left(\prod _{i=1} ^{n} {x_i} 
  \right)^{1/n} =\ \sqrt[n] {x_1 \ x_2 \cdots x_n }
}
$$
