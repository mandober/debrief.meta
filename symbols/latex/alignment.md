# Alignment

- wrap in alignment tags: `\begin{align}` and `\end{align}`
- put `&` in front of symbol, in each row, you want aligned vertically


#### align q's

$$
\begin{align}
  s \land r \lor  & q \lor t \rightarrow u           \\
  p \land         & q \rightarrow p                       \\
                  & q \land s
\end{align}
$$

#### aligner

$$
\begin{align}
&10^{-12} &=& 0.00000000000        \\
&10^{-9}  &=& 0.00000000        \\
&10^{-6}  &=& 0.000001       \\
&10^{-3}  &=& 0.001       \\
&10^{-2}  &=& 0.01      \\
&10^{-1}  &=& 0.1     \\
&10^0     &=& 1     \\
&10^{1}   &=& 10      \\
&10^{2}   &=& 100       \\
&10^{3}   &=& 1,000       \\
&10^{6}   &=& 1,000,000       \\
&10^{9}   &=& 1,000,000,000       \\
&10^{12}  &=& 1,000,000,000,000
\end{align}
$$



#### alignment and tag

$$
\begin{align}
(a+b)^2 &= (a+b)(a+b)               & \tag{3.1c}       \\
        &= a^2 + ab + ba + b^2      & \tag{$\dagger$}  \\
        &= a^2 + 2ab + b^2          & \tag{$\ast$}
\end{align}
$$
